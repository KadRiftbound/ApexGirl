Frequently Asked Questions

What is TopGirl / ApexGirl?

TopGirl (also ApexGirl) is a mobile game by A3Games featuring idol collection, rhythm gameplay, and strategy. Players collect and train artists to build the ultimate idol team.

How to get started?

Complete the tutorial, collect initial artists through free pulls, and build a balanced team. Focus on upgrading your main team before spreading resources.

What are the best artists?

UR-ranked artists are generally strongest, but SSR and SR artists can be excellent with right builds. Use our database to compare all artists.

Is TopGirl free to play?

Yes, completely free-to-play. Game provides free currency through daily logins and gameplay. Paid currency can speed up progress but isn't required.
