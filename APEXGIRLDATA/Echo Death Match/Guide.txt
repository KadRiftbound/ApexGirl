Echo Deathmatch

Type
Weekly Challenge Event

Short Explanation

Echo Deathmatch takes place every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday and lasts 3 days.
Before the event starts, players must choose a difficulty level.
This choice cannot be changed during the event.

The goal is to defeat stages and collect rewards.

Long Explanation

Before the event begins, players must select a difficulty level. Only one difficulty can be played during the event.

Each event contains 15 stages. Every stage appears for 1 hour. If a stage disappears, it can be summoned again.

Players must summon a rock stage and start a challenge. Victory allows you to continue to the next stage.

Defeating a stage grants basic rewards, similar to Concert Performance rewards.

Additional rewards are available after defeating milestone stages:

Stage 3

Stage 6

Stage 9

Stage 12

Stage 15

Completing the challenge at your current difficulty allows you to attempt a higher difficulty in the next event.

Higher difficulty levels provide better rewards.

Tips

Choose the right difficulty. Select a level you can complete fully.

Play regularly during the event. Each stage lasts only 1 hour.

Clear stages quickly so you do not miss the next one.

Track milestone stages to claim the additional rewards.

Rewards

Players can obtain:

Basic Rewards

- Rewards similar to Concert Performance rewards

Milestone Rewards

- SR Private Photos

- SSR Private Photos

- SSR Cards

- EXP Cards

The amount of rewards depend on the difficulty level and stage progression.
